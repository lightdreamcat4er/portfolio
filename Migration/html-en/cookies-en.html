Cookies Policy

The use of cookies by Foreigners Lublin

Cookies may be used on the websites and online services of Foreigners Lublin. Cookies allow you to personalize your browsing experience on our sites. They help us track the most visited web pages, measure the effectiveness of advertisements and internet searches, and provide insight into user behavior so that we can improve our communications and products.

Cookies are small pieces of information that a website places on a user's computer. These small files enable the server to provide you with information tailored to your needs the next time you use a platform or website. Cookies are used to improve your website experience. These include, for example, cookies that help manage the statistics that the website owner maintains. But this is not the only useful function of cookies.

Also, cookies are tools that enable behavioral advertising. If you have searched for any product or service and a particular company offers such products or services, cookies on your computer may help that company offer their products and services to you. Thanks to cookies, the owner of the website or platform can see the actions and preferences of the user on the website or platform, analyze this information and provide the user with more relevant information regarding the services that he is using now or will use in the future. Some services collect statistics in a generalized form and may not require user consent, some services are operated directly by the owner of the website and - depending on the description - without the help of third parties.

If you have visited our Website, then most likely the first message that you saw was a message about consent to the use of cookies. It was this that notified you of the use of cookies on our Website. In most cases, we ask you to consent to the setting of cookies. This does not apply to strictly necessary cookies, such as, for example, technical cookies. The data that we collect through cookies is processed in accordance with the provisions of our Privacy Policy.

For example, our employees, contractors and affiliates may have access to this data. We transfer data to them on the basis of standard contractual clauses adopted by the European Commission. We also use Google Analytics when we set cookies and use the data collected through them. That is, we may also transfer such data to Google LLC, which is a US company. They adhere to the requirements of the regulation of cross-border data transfer between the EU and the USA - EU-US Privacy Shield. To monitor the collection of data for analytical purposes by Google Analytics, please visit this website: Google Analytics revocation page. We keep the data collected using cookies for 5 years. At the end of this period, we make all such data anonymous and store it solely for statistical purposes.

Can you revoke your consent and turn off the collection of cookies?

Yes, you can do this by selecting the appropriate settings in your browser.
