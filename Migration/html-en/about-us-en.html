About Us

We provide legal services in the field of migration for foreigners in Ukraine. From the beginning of the launch of our practice, in 2014, and until today, we have helped thousands of foreigners with documents and various legal aspects related to their legal immigration to Ukraine. Our multi-lingual and highly qualified team is always able to find the necessary legal solutions for each specific case. We are guided by the principles of the rule of law, mutual respect, tolerance and honesty. We provide legal services on the basis of contracts, which set out all the conditions for the provision of services and payment procedures. The founder of the company is Vladyslav Yevdokimov - one of the leading migration lawyers of Ukraine with many years of experience in daily work with foreigners. We are proud to have grateful clients from more than 80 countries of the world whom we have already helped with documents in Ukraine. And the geography of appeals to us is continuously expanding.
