Services

Temporary Residence Permit

Looks like a plastic card. It may be issued for 1 or 3 years and entitles a foreigner to stay in Ukraine for the entire period of validity of a temporary residence permit. This document gives you the right to cross the border of Ukraine an unlimited number of times. Unlike with the permanent residence permit, it is much easier to find grounds for obtaining a temporary one.

Permanent Residence Permit

A document that allows a foreigner to become a fully-fledged resident of Ukraine. Its holders don't need to renew or prolong it after a year or three. Once it is issued, it remains valid forever. Permanent residence permit gives you the right to work in Ukraine without receiving a separate work permit. Also, it looks like a plastic card.

Ukrainian Citizenship

Ukrainian passport allows you to travel to nearly a hundred without visa, or with visa upon arrival. You will not need a visa to visit almost all countries of the European Union. And of course, a Ukrainian passport gives you the right to live, work, study here. And also get a lot of other benefits.
